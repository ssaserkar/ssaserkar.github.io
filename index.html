Projects

  • Autonomous Dynamic Obstacle Navigation in ROS Husky

    Developed and implemented global planners, including OP-PRM, RRTx, and Risk-DTRRT, using ROS and C++. Integrated local planners, DWA and TEB from the Husky Local Planner. Conducted extensive testing and debugging to ensure the robustness and accuracy.

  • Panoptic Segmentation on 3D LiDAR Point Cloud Data

    Converted the 3D Point Cloud Image to 2D Spherical Projection to reduce dimensionality. Utilized the encoder-decoder of the RangeNet++ Architecture to obtain relevant feature maps. Remodeled the Semantic Head of RangeNet++ for Semantic Segmentation, and used Mask-RCNN for Instance Segmentation. Implemented the Panoptic Head, having features of Mask Matching and Mask Removal.

  • Automating the Endoscope Camera of the DVRK

    Automated the Endoscope camera for the daVinci Research Kit, tracked gaze within Unity3D. Performed Semantic Segmentation on the CholecSeg8k using the UNet++ Architecture. Achieved an overall accuracy of 68% and reduced training time by using the pretrained resnet18 weights. Region of Interest within the surgical frame by using the semantically segmented image.

  • Development of a Delta Wing Glider prototype as a payload

    Mentored a team of 10 to develop a delta-wing glider as a payload to deploy from a can-sized satellite for CANSAT 2020. Developed an active feedback sensor-actuator interface for ailerons to maintain a tilt of 3Â° and a helical trajectory of 250m. Ranked 53rd globally and came in the 88th percentile.
